# Equity Capital Markets (ECM)

ECM is the area of investment banking focused on the issuance of equity (stock).

- **IPO**: Initial Public Offering — first time a company sells stock to the public.
- **Follow-on Offering**: Issuing more shares after an IPO.
- **Convertible Bonds**: Bonds that can be converted into equity.
- **Role of the Underwriter**: Managing the sale and price discovery.
