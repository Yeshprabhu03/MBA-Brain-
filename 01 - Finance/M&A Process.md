# M&A Process

The lifecycle of a merger or acquisition deal.

1. **Strategic Planning**: Identifying targets or buyers.
2. **First Round**: Teaser, NDA, CIM (Confidential Information Memorandum).
3. **Second Round**: Data room access, management presentations, IOIs (Indications of Interest).
4. **Negotiation & Closing**: Definitive Agreement, regulatory approval, day-one integration.
