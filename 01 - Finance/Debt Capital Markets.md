# Debt Capital Markets (DCM)

DCM refers to the market where companies and governments raise funds through the issuance of debt securities like bonds.

- **Investment Grade**: High credit quality (AAA to BBB-).
- **High Yield (Junk Bonds)**: Lower credit quality (BB+ and below).
- **Key Players**: Investment banks, institutional investors, credit rating agencies.
- **Tools**: Bonds, commercial paper, syndicated loans.
