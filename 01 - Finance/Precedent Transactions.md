# Precedent Transactions

A valuation method that looks at the prices paid for similar companies in past M&A deals.

- **Transaction Multiples**: e.g., EV/EBITDA.
- **Control Premium**: M&A deals usually include a premium (20-30%) over market price.
- **Synergies**: Buyers pay more if they can extract operational or financial value.
- **Context Matters**: Cycle timing impacts multiples.
