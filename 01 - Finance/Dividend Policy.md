# Dividend Policy

The strategy used by a company to determine the amount and timing of dividend payments to shareholders.

- **Signaling Theory**: Dividends signal management's confidence in future cash flows.
- **Tax Effect**: Preference of capital gains vs. dividends.
- **Residual Dividend Model**: Pay dividends only after all positive NPV projects are funded.
- **Share Buybacks**: An alternative to dividends that can be more tax-efficient.
