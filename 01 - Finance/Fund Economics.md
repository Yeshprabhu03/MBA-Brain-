# Fund Economics

The financial structure of private equity and venture capital funds.

- **2 and 20**: Standard fee structure (2% management fee, 20% carried interest).
- **Carried Interest**: The share of profits paid to the general partners (GPs).
- **Hurdle Rate**: The minimum return the fund must achieve before GPs receive carry.
- **Clawback**: A provision allowing limited partners to reclaim carry if later performance dips.
