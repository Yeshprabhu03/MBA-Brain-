# Sum of the Parts (SOTP) Valuation

Valuing a conglomerate by valuing each of its business segments separately.

- **Process**: Value each division using comps/DCF and then add them up.
- **Conglomerate Discount**: The trend where the market values a conglomerate at less than the sum of its parts.
- **Strategic Use**: Identifying businesses that might be worth more if spun off.
