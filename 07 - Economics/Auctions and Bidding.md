# Auctions and Bidding

The process of buying and selling goods or services by offering them up for bid.

- **English Auction**: Ascending price.
- **Dutch Auction**: Descending price.
- **First-Price Sealed-Bid**: Highest bidder wins and pays their bid.
- **Vickrey Auction (Second-Price)**: Highest bidder wins but pays the second-highest bid.
- **Winner's Curse**: Winning an auction by overestimating the true value of the item.
