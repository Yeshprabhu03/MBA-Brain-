# Divestiture Strategy

The partial or full disposal of a business unit through sale, exchange, closure, or bankruptcy.

- **Rationale**: Slimming down to the core business, raising capital, or removing a low-performing asset.
- **Types**:
    - **Spin-off**: Creating a new independent company.
    - **Equity Carve-out**: Sale of minority stake via IPO.
    - **Trade Sale**: Sale to another corporation.
