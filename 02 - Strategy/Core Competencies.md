# Core Competencies

A concept by Prahalad and Hamel describes a harmonized combination of multiple resources and skills that distinguish a firm in the marketplace.

- **Criteria**:
    1. Provides potential access to a wide variety of markets.
    2. Makes a significant contribution to the perceived customer benefits.
    3. Difficult for competitors to imitate.
- **Focus**: Strategic management should focus on nurturing core competencies rather than just individual products.
