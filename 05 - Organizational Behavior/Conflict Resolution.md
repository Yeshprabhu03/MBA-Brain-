# Conflict Resolution

Methods for facilitating the peaceful ending of conflict and retribution.

- **Thomas-Kilmann Model (TKI)**:
    - **Competing**: High assertiveness, low cooperation.
    - **Collaborating**: High assertiveness, high cooperation (Win-Win).
    - **Compromising**: Middle ground.
    - **Avoiding**: Low assertiveness, low cooperation.
    - **Accommodating**: Low assertiveness, high cooperation.
