# Change Management

The structured approach to transitioning individuals, teams, and organizations from a current state to a desired future state.

- **Kotter's 8 Steps**: Sense of urgency -> Guiding coalition -> Vision -> Communicate -> Empower -> Short-term wins -> Consolidation -> Anchoring in culture.
- **Lewin's Model**: Unfreeze -> Change -> Refreeze.
- **Resistance**: Understanding and mitigating inertia and fear of change.
