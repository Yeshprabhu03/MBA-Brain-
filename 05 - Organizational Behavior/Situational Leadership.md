# Situational Leadership

A model (Hersey-Blanchard) suggesting that no single leading style is best; instead, it depends on the situation and the maturity/competence of the followers.

- **Directing**: For low competence, high commitment.
- **Coaching**: For some competence, low commitment.
- **Supporting**: For high competence, variable commitment.
- **Delegating**: For high competence, high commitment.
