# 📚 Case Studies — Map of Content

> The case method forces you to make decisions with incomplete information under time pressure — exactly like real management. HBS alone produces ~500 new cases/year.

*"You are not here to learn what to think. You are here to learn how to think."* — HBS Case Method

---

## 📖 How to Read a Case (HBS Method)

1. **Read the case end to beginning** — read exhibits first, then the case, then questions
2. **Identify the decision** — what specific decision must the protagonist make?
3. **Identify the constraints** — time, capital, information, political
4. **Analyze** — use relevant frameworks
5. **Make a recommendation** — be decisive; hedge = bad
6. **Prepare 3 discussion points** — what will you contribute in class?
