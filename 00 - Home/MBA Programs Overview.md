# 🏫 MBA Programs Overview

A comparison of the six elite MBA programs this vault maps knowledge from.

---

## Why These Six?

These programs consistently rank at the very top globally and each has carved out **distinct intellectual identities** — different pedagogies, faculty strengths, and industry pipelines.

| School | Known For | Signature Pedagogy |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| [[HBS\|Harvard Business School]] | Leadership, General Management | Case Method (95% cases) |
| [[Wharton\|Wharton School, UPenn]] | Finance, Analytics | Quantitative rigor |
| [[Booth\|Chicago Booth]] | Economics-based thinking | Econometrics, Capital Markets |
| [[Kellogg\|Kellogg School]] | Marketing, Teamwork | Experiential learning |
| [[Stanford GSB\|Stanford GSB]] | Entrepreneurship, VC | Self-reflection, Leadership |
| [[Columbia\|Columbia Business School]] | Finance, Value Investing | Wall Street proximity |

---

## Harvard Business School (HBS)

- **Location**: Boston, MA
- **Class size**: ~930 students
- **Tuition**: ~$115,000/year
- **Signature courses**: LEAD, Finance I & II, Strategy, TOM, FRC
- **Famous alumni**: Michael Bloomberg, Mitt Romney, Sheryl Sandberg
- **Strengths**: General management, PE, consulting, CPG
- **Note**: Almost entirely case-based; readings are long; cold-calling is standard

---

## Wharton School (UPenn)

- **Location**: Philadelphia, PA
- **Class size**: ~900 students
- **Tuition**: ~$115,000/year
- **Signature courses**: Corporate Valuation, MGEC, STAT, Legal Studies
- **Famous alumni**: Elon Musk, Warren Buffett (briefly), Sundar Pichai
- **Strengths**: Finance, consulting, healthcare, real estate
- **Note**: Most analytically rigorous of the M7; strong quant finance pipeline

---

## Chicago Booth

- **Location**: Chicago, IL (+ London, Hong Kong)
- **Class size**: ~580 students
- **Tuition**: ~$113,000/year
- **Signature courses**: Financial Accounting, Econometrics, Capital Markets
- **Famous alumni**: Eugene Fama, David Booth, Satya Nadella
- **Strengths**: Finance, economics, trading, asset management
- **Note**: Most flexible curriculum; Nobel laureates on faculty; Chicago School of Economics tradition

---

## Kellogg School (Northwestern)

- **Location**: Evanston, IL
- **Class size**: ~530 students
- **Tuition**: ~$112,000/year
- **Signature courses**: Marketing Management, Negotiations, Leadership
- **Famous alumni**: Jim Cramer, Sanjay Jha
- **Strengths**: Marketing, consulting, healthcare, consumer goods
- **Note**: Collaborative culture; heavy team-based learning; top for marketing globally

---

## Stanford GSB

- **Location**: Stanford, CA
- **Class size**: ~420 students (most selective MBA globally)
- **Tuition**: ~$117,000/year
- **Signature courses**: Touchy-Feely (GSBGEN 259), Strategic Leadership, Startup Garage
- **Famous alumni**: Phil Knight, Reid Hoffman, Mary Barra
- **Strengths**: Entrepreneurship, VC, tech, impact investing
- **Note**: "Know yourself to lead others" philosophy; deep Silicon Valley ties

---

## Columbia Business School

- **Location**: New York City, NY
- **Class size**: ~750 students
- **Tuition**: ~$115,000/year
- **Signature courses**: Security Analysis, Value Investing, Applied Value Investing
- **Famous alumni**: Warren Buffett (studied here under Graham), Henry Kravis, Vikram Pandit
- **Strengths**: Value investing, hedge funds, asset management, IB
- **Note**: Only M7 school in NYC; home of Ben Graham's value investing tradition
