## 🧠 Philosophy: Chicago School of Economics

Booth is anchored in the **Chicago School of Economics** — a tradition built on free markets, rational agents, and empirical rigor.

Core beliefs:
- Markets are generally efficient (Fama's EMH)
- Price signals contain information
- Government intervention often creates inefficiency
- Empirical evidence should override intuition

This makes Booth the most **intellectually challenging** and **economics-driven** MBA program.

## 📚 Curriculum: Maximum Flexibility

Booth has the **most flexible curriculum** of any top MBA:
- Only a handful of required courses
- Students design their own curriculum
- Can even waive required courses with placement exams
- Flex MBA (part-time) and Executive MBA options highly regarded

## 💰 Finance Specialization

Booth competes with Wharton for #1 in finance:
- Nobel Prize winners Eugene Fama, Lars Peter Hansen, Robert Lucas
- BUSF 35150 (Advanced Investments) — legendary course
- Center for Research in Security Prices (CRSP) — foundational finance database
- Strong trading, asset management, and hedge fund placement
