## 🎭 Philosophy: Know Yourself to Lead Others

Unlike HBS (case method) or Wharton (quant rigor), GSB is obsessed with **self-awareness as the foundation of leadership**.

Core philosophy:
> *"I have sat with many of the world's most powerful leaders... The ones who seem most effective are those who know themselves deeply."* — Robert Joss, former GSB Dean

## 🤝 "Touchy Feely" — GSBGEN 259

The most famous MBA course in the world. A non-negotiable part of the curriculum.
- Small T-groups of 12 students
- Raw, unfiltered interpersonal feedback
- No cases, no models — pure human connection
- Students say it's "the most transformative thing they've ever done"
- Influenced by NTL sensitivity training; exists since 1968

---

## 🚀 Startup Garage & Venture Programs

- **Startup Garage**: 2-quarter experiential course where teams build real companies
- **Stanford d.school (Hasso Plattner Institute)**: Design Thinking methodology
- **GSB Social Innovation Fellowship**: Paid fellowships for social enterprise
- Proximity to **Sand Hill Road** (world's VC capital) is unmatched
