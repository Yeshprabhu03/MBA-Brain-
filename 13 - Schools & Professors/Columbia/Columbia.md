## 💰 Value Investing Legacy

Columbia is the spiritual home of **value investing** — Benjamin Graham taught here and mentored Warren Buffett.

### The Graham & Dodd Tradition:
- Benjamin Graham wrote *Security Analysis* (1934) here — the foundational text
- Warren Buffett studied under Graham at Columbia (1950–51)
- **Applied Value Investing** course: One of most coveted electives
- **Heilbrunn Center for Graham & Dodd Investing** — hosts annual Graham & Dodd Breakfast
- Students pitch stocks to fund managers with real capital
