## 🏛️ Pedagogy: The Case Method

HBS invented the case method and uses it for **~95% of classroom instruction**.

**How it works**:
- Students read a 15–30 page case the night before
- 80-person sections debate the case with cold-calling
- No "right answer" — the point is developing judgment under ambiguity
- Professor plays devil's advocate; debate is the learning

**Famous cases originated at HBS**:
- Lincoln Electric (compensation design)
- Marriott Corporation Cost of Capital (WACC)
- Intel's Strategic Inflection Point (strategy)
- Netflix (disruption and content strategy)
