## 🤝 Philosophy: Collaboration + Leadership

Kellogg stands out for its **team-based, collaborative culture** — which contrasts sharply with more competitive environments at HBS or Wharton.

Core traits:
- Heavy emphasis on **teamwork** — almost all projects are group-based
- Best-in-class for **marketing** (Philip Kotler's home)
- Strong focus on **healthcare management**
- Known for producing warm, supportive cohorts

## 📣 Marketing Supremacy

Kellogg is **globally ranked #1 in Marketing** and has been for decades:
- Philip Kotler taught here for 50+ years ("father of modern marketing")
- Derek Rucker — advertising and psychology of marketing
- Bobby Calder — marketing strategy and consumer insight
- Famous cases in consumer goods, CPG, brand strategy

---

## 💉 Healthcare Management

Kellogg + Northwestern's Feinberg School of Medicine = unrivaled MD/MBA pathway:
- Joint MD/MBA degree offered
- Health Enterprise Management Certificate
- Major consulting recruiters focus here for healthcare clients
