## 🔢 Philosophy: Analytical Rigor

Wharton is the most **quantitatively rigorous** of the M7. If HBS is about judgment and leadership, Wharton is about analysis and data.

Core traits:
- Economics and statistics are taken seriously
- Strong emphasis on empirical research in finance and marketing
- "The Wharton Alumni Network" is considered the most powerful in business

## 💰 Finance Dominance

Wharton is **#1 for finance globally** by almost every measure:
- Most Wall Street finance professionals hold Wharton degrees
- FNCE 236 (Valuation) and FNCE 728 (Advanced Investments) are legendary
- Heavy PE, hedge fund, and investment banking placement
- Wharton professors have written defining finance textbooks (Brealey-Myers)
