# Bullwhip Effect

A distribution channel phenomenon in which forecasts yield supply chain inefficiencies as one moves further upstream.

- **Cause**: Small fluctuations in demand at the retail level can cause progressively larger fluctuations at the wholesale, distributor, and manufacturer levels.
- **Drivers**: Lack of communication, disorganization, price variations.
- **Mitigation**: Information sharing, vendor-managed inventory, reducing lead times.
