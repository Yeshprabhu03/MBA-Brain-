## 🔑 Core Concepts & Formulas

| Concept | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| Throughput | `Output / Time` |
| Cycle Time | `1 / Throughput` |
| Utilization | `Demand Rate / Capacity` |
| Little's Law | `WIP = Throughput × Cycle Time` |
| EOQ | `√(2DS/H)` |
| Safety Stock | `z × σ_LT × √LT` |
| OEE | `Availability × Performance × Quality` |
