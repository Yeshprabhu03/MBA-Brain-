# Quality-Cost Tradeoff

The balance between the cost of achieving quality and the cost of poor quality.

- **Prevention Costs**: Costs to prevent defects (training, maintenance).
- **Appraisal Costs**: Costs to detect defects (testing, inspections).
- **Internal Failure Costs**: Defects found before the customer (rework, scrap).
- **External Failure Costs**: Defects found by the customer (warranty, recall).
