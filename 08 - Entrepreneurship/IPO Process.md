# IPO Process

The steps to take a private company public.

1. **Selection**: Hiring underwriters (Investment Banks).
2. **Due Diligence & Filings**: S-1 filing with the SEC.
3. **Roadshow**: Pitching to institutional investors for 2 weeks.
4. **Pricing & Allocation**: Setting the final offer price and distributing shares.
5. **Trading**: Day one on the exchange.
