# Effectuation Theory

A way of thinking used by expert entrepreneurs to make decisions in highly uncertain environments (Saras Sarasvathy).

- **Principles**:
    - **Bird in Hand**: Start with your means (who you are, what you know, who you know).
    - **Affordable Loss**: Focus on the downside risk.
    - **Crazy Quilt**: Build partnerships.
    - **Lemonade**: Leverage contingencies/surprises.
